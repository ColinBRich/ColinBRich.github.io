Artist Bio

Tennessee singer-songwriter Izzy Arthurs commands attention with raw authenticity and a magnetic stage presence, captivating audiences with her genre-bending sound. Backed by an elite 4-piece band, Arthurs weaves intimate storytelling with alternative rock and indie-pop sensibilities. Her performances are an electric intersection where vulnerability meets power — turning concert halls into sacred spaces where even the back row feels pulled into her orbit. With each show, she proves herself as an artist who doesn't just perform, she creates moments of genuine connection with her audience in an eruption of adrenaline, sweat, glitter, and joy.
